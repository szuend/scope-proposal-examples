05 - Inlining across modules

Inlining of multiple functions into a module scope. The example aims to show case where the scope chain of the inlined function differs from the scope chain where it got inlined. 

google-closure-compiler --compilation_level ADVANCED inline_across_modules.js module.js --js_output_file inline_across_modules.min.js --create_source_map inline_across_modules.min.js.map --source_map_include_content --language_in UNSTABLE
